What we want to achieve:

Researching and understanding challenges of communities To achieve our vision we see it as our mission to investigate and understand issues causing racial/ethnic divides within diverse communities. We strive to understand local cultures and the pain points surrounding integration for minority/migrant populations. Explore and exhibit different cultures to expand understanding We use our platforms as safe spaces to encourage cultural exploration. Taking a deeper look at the traditions and beliefs of different populations living together will inspire a better understanding of our neighbours and create a more empathetic society. Give back to marginalised and most in need

We will also form strong partnerships with local and global organisations focused on making long-term change within the communities we work in, and the cultures we explore and exhibit. We will lobby governments and other powerful groups to fight racial injustice by creating policies that work to support marginalised communities. Eliminate all forms of racism and bigotry We hope through our work we will ultimately defuse and eliminate bigotry and racism at all levels to bring about a deeper level of understanding of communities across the world. Create an inclusive world
